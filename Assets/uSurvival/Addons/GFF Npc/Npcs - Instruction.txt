                                      Installation and Configuration

1. Drag the "GFF NpcTrading.prefab" to the canvas element so canvas is the parent.

2. Select the "NpcDialogue" object. In the inspector
        Drag in "Npc Trading panel" the "GFF NpcTrading" in Canvas\GFF NpcTrading


3. Configure the advanced inventory addon



	                                  important
to expand the functionality, configure the addon "GFF Inventory Extended" and Enable "use Trading System Addon "
